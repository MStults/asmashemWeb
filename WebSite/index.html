What is Atom Smashem?

Atom Smashem is a different kind of matching game that removes the limits of just swapping pieces. Atom Smashem allows you to move a piece wherever you want! The goal is to arrange like atoms together to start a chain reaction. Experience the fun of discovering what each atom does as it increases in energy. Each atom has its own unique ability that can only be unlocked when it reaches its maximum energy level. There are unique new features such as clamps and pipes that you will have to master to complete the challenges and beat the higher levels. There are 328 levels that take place in 3 different reactors which is a whole lot of fun all for free.
Enjoy!

atom Mikeium 300ev

Produces a very high amount of energy. Only the atom LIGHTNINGIUM can match this atom's energy output. Plus its isotope is very useful when advancing other atoms energy levels.

isotope Doppelgangorgen 250eV

Isotope of MIKEIUM. This atom has the ability to clone all adjacent atoms including itself into the atom that triggered it. Often, this results in the creation of LIGHTNINGIUM.

atom Jasonium 150ev

This atom and its isotope produce the least amount of energy of all the atoms. But, do not let it fool you because its isotope is very broad, quick, and powerful!

isotope Shotgunium 150ev

Isotope of JASONIUM. This atom unleashes its nucleus in the direction of the atom that triggered it. It produces a devastating effect that can easily destroy several atoms releasing lots of energy.

atom Russium 175ev

This atom yields a small amount of energy, but more than JASONIUM. It has an isotope that is sure to strike!

isotope Missilenium 200ev

Isotope of RUSSIUM. This atom has the unique ability to target all atoms of a specific type. Just trigger it with the type of atom you want to destroy and MISSILENIUM will do the rest.

atom Claytonium 225ev

Claytonium produces an average amount of energy and has an isotope that is very ornate and fun to watch.

isotope Lasergen 250ev

Isotope of CLAYTONIUM. It shoots a narrow beam of high energy waves along its horizontal or vertical axes depending on the direction of the triggering atom. This beam will disintegrate anything in its path. However, this triggering atom will remain unscathed.

Atom joedrogen 275ev

Odd looking atom that produces a high amount of energy. Its isotope is both devastating and gross.

isotope Antimattergen 225ev

This is an isotope of JOEDROGEN, but it acts more like antimatter. When this atom is triggered it creeps along a path destroying any atom that it comes in contact with. The higher its energy level-the further it creeps.

Atom danthemanium 275ev

Two blue electrons combine together to produce a whopping 275ev. This atom can be a real game changer, as it can go the distance and produce a 'win' when your in a pinch.

isotope minesium 175ev

Isotope of DANTHEMANIUM. Triggering this atom results in a small explosion that instantly causes the triggering atom to 'phase change' into LIGHTNINGIUM. This can be incredibly useful when creating isotope of a specific type.

atom Lightningium 300ev

Cool fact-All atoms have the potential of becoming LIGHTNINGIUM. LIGHTNINGIUM comes into existence when an atom reaches the forth energy level. This is the only atom that can be triggered just by clicking on it. AND when this atom is activated it sends out electrical energy in all directions resulting in the creation of an isotope.

atom IRON 200ev

Iron is a very stable atom that acts like a barrier. It does not react with its self like the other atoms do but it can be destroyed.

The Basics

The goal of Atom Smashem is to start a chain-reaction by raising the total energy in a reactor to its critical level. To do this, arrange atoms in sets of three or more. An atom can be move to any adjacent space occupied by another atom. The other atom will then be triggered.

Moving Atoms

You can move an atom by either by clicking on it and dragging it left, right, up or down. Or you can simply swipe it in the direction you want it to move. Atoms cannot move diagonally.

how to win

Maximize the engergy level by triggering isotopes OR arrange atoms in sets of 3 or more. CAUTION: creating mismatches will decrease the overall ENERGY LEVEL. Continue reading to learn how to maximize energy.

Energy Levels

To release energy from an atom it has to be put into an unstable state. The atom becomes more unstable as its energy level increases. The energy level increases every time it is used in a '3 of a kind' match.

Level 1 - Also known as normal state. The atom has no electrons, and all of its orbits are empty. In this state the atom will not yield much energy.

Level 2 - Atom used in a '3 of a kind' match. The atom has all of its electrons and will yield the maximum amount of energy.

Level 3 - Repeated action of '3 of a kind' match. The atom is close to breaking down into its plasma state.

Level 4 - Repeated action of '3 of a kind' match. The excess energy causes the atom to undergo a 'phase change' turning it into an isotope called Lightingium.

isotopes

The creation of isotopes are dependent on the type of atom that was used to create the Lightningium; for example, if Mikeium atoms were used to create a Lightningium the resulting isotope would be a Doppelgangorgen.

Play Smart

Speed is not a factor so take your time and pick matches that will result in higher energy levels.
